IN ENGLISH

I am running for parliament because I want to fight for genuine social justice.

In the upcoming years we must solve the climate crisis, do as much as we can to eliminate inequality and human rights violations, and secure an equal education for all on all educational levels.

For way too long our country has been run by short-sighted middle-aged cis hetero men, who have gone out of their ways to pay no mind to the climate crisis and the voice of the future generations. It is about time we put an end to this!

I am determined to create a better future, although this cannot be done single-handedly. I have a great team behind me, and we would love for you to join!

We welcome all the help we can get. You can support my journey towards becoming a member of parliament joining our amazing team or donating to the campaign.

I wholeheartedly invite you to join me and my team on this mission to create an environmentally friendly and equitable future!
